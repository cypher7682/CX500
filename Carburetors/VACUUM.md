# Carburetor Vacuums

## Troubleshooting low Vacuum

If vacuum on one side is low, there's a few things that might be wrong. Check through the diagnostic list:

- [] Vacuum low at any RPM
- [] Vacuum low only at idle
- [] Check dry compression on both sides hot.
- [] Check wet compression on both sides hot.
- [] Back fire *into carbs*
- [] Back fire *into exhaust*

There's [will be] a flow chart below to cover bases to figure out what's what.

- Intake leak
- Head gasket failure
- Valve clearance
- Valve seating
